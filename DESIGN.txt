Genre: Fighting game
Title: Mediocre Road Warrior
Goal: 
	The player advances levels by defeating enemies. 
	Each level is a fight against a different enemy, and the player wins by defeating them all.
	Defeat an enemy by reducing its HP to zero. 
	If the player dies, game over.
Mechanics: 
	Use the arrow keys to move and ASD for attacks.
	Combinations of keys launch different attacks, with different properties.
	Block by holding back, jump by pressing up.
Levels: 
	Each level is against a different enemy, each of whom has different moves and/or AI
