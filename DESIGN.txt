Genre: Fighting game
Title: Mediocre Road Warrior
Goal: 
	2-Player fight: a player wins by defeating the opponent and loses if defeated.
	Defeat an opponent by reducing their HP to zero or doing enough damage before time runs out.
Mechanics: 
	Player 1 uses WASD to move and FGH for attacks.
	Player 2 uses arrow keys to move and IOP for attacks.
	Combinations of keys launch different attacks, with different properties.
	Jump by pressing up, up-forward, or up-back.
	Crouch by pressing down or down-back.
Levels/Modes: 
	There is a standard stock mode where you defeat the opponent by reducing their health to zero.
	There is also a timed mode where you defeat the opponent by doing more damage than them.
